This model provides an aggregated view of customers, combining data from customers and stores tables. 
It displays store data enriched with the following :
- **customer location**: The city the customer is located at.
- **total_customers**: The total number of customer in each recorded city.

It provides a comprehensive view of customers per location, aimed at facilitating analysis of local consumers related to store location.
